1. Why this app should be used ?

In today’s digitally changing time company has developed this app to get rid of paper survey system. It saves printing cost, distribution and collection cost, data entry cost and above all it saves valuable time. If by some reason any value is added or updated then it can be implemented immediately in this app while it is not possible in paper survey system. All work need to be done again which will double the cost.

With this app all survey results can be shown immediately which is not possible in paper survey system. So in comparison to paper survey system it definitely saves users time and money.

Before or after announcement of election any political person or party can conduct survey using this app. They can assess their position and make effective strategies time to time by knowing people opinion on mentioned different categories and questions.
